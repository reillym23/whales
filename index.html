Whales

Whales are some of the largest animals on Earth. They live in the oean and have flippers, but some may be surprised to hear that they are mammals just like you and me. Whales can have teeth or a special mouthpart called baleen. Whales like the sperm whale have teeth and humpack whales have baleen.
